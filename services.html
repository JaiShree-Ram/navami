Preserving Sanatan Dharma Through Seva and Community Efforts

  • Sanatan Trust works through several sacred initiatives to protect temples, organize spiritual festivals, and strengthen the traditions of Sanatan Dharma across India. Our services are dedicated to creating spiritual awareness, supporting temple communities, and bringing devotees together through faith and devotion.

Festival & Community Service

Festival Celebration Support

Sanatan Trust organizes and supports sacred temple festivals across India so devotees can experience the joy and spiritual energy of Sanatan traditions through grand celebrations and community devotion.

Resources for Grand Celebrations

With the support of devotees and volunteers, we provide essential resources for temples to conduct large Mahotsavs, encouraging community participation and spiritual unity.

Our Festival Support Includes

Temple decorations and lighting, puja materials, ritual preparation, devotional bhajans, and spiritual gatherings that bring communities together in devotion.

Education & Community Seva

Educational Support

Sanatan Trust supports educational initiatives that help students receive knowledge along with cultural values rooted in Sanatan Dharma.

Community Welfare Programs

We organize social initiatives and awareness programs that promote compassion, unity, and service within communities.

Volunteer & Devotee Participation

Devotees across India participate through seva, volunteering, and donations to support temples, festivals, and community programs.
