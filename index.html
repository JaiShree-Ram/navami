Protecting Sanatan Dharma Heritage

Sanatan Trust is a spiritual initiative devoted to protecting the sacred heritage of Sanatan Dharma. Our mission is to restore temples, celebrate ancient traditions, and inspire devotion in communities across India. Festivals such as Gudi Padwa, the beginning of the Hindu New Year and the sacred month of Chaitra, remind us of renewal, prosperity, and divine blessings. Another powerful celebration is Ram Navami, the birth anniversary of Lord Rama, symbolizing righteousness, courage, and the victory of truth over evil. These festivals connect millions of devotees to the timeless values of Sanatan culture.With your generous support, we can restore these sacred places and keep the flame of devotion alive for future generations. Together, we can protect temples, celebrate sacred traditions, and strengthen the spiritual identity of Sanatan society.
