Guided by Faith, Dedicated to Sanatan Heritage

Sanatan Trust brings together devotees, volunteers, and communities to protect sacred traditions and strengthen the spiritual foundation of society. Through devotion, service, and collective participation, we are building a movement that preserves the spiritual identity of our nation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote spiritual awareness, devotion, and service while preserving the sacred values of Sanatan Dharma. We actively support temple restoration, organize spiritual events, and create opportunities for devotees to participate in seva that strengthens faith and community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a spiritually awakened society where temples flourish, traditions thrive, and the wisdom of Sanatan Dharma guides every generation. We envision communities across India united by devotion, compassion, and cultural pride.
