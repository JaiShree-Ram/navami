Spiritual Gatherings and Celebrations

Gudi Padwa marks the beginning of the Hindu New Year and symbolizes prosperity, victory, and spiritual renewal. On this sacred day, devotees raise the Gudi flag, visit temples, and offer prayers for blessings and success. Your valuable donation helps organize the Gudi Padwa celebrations, support temple activities, and bring the community together in devotion. Contribute with faith and help preserve this sacred tradition for future generations.
